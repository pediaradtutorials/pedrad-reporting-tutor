Clinical Disclaimer: This tool is intended to support — not replace — clinical judgment. Diagnosis of MS must be made by a qualified physician who integrates clinical findings, imaging, and laboratory results in the appropriate patient context. Always exclude better explanations before applying these criteria.

Core Diagnostic Principle

The 2024 criteria require: (1) Typical lesions in characteristic CNS locations; (2) No better explanation. A diagnosis of MS is always one of exclusion — NMOSD and MOGAD must be ruled out, especially with optic neuritis or longitudinally extensive myelitis.
